About China:

1. What is your view about China? Do you see a new culture rising regarding art? Also, what do you think about the Chinese young artists that are coming up?

I had amazing experience in China through the different projects and shows I did there in the past few years..the latest project Inside Out was a great way for me to really involve the population... I was really surprise how many people did participate and how much streets got covered!

2. Which Asian artist do you admire?

I collaborated with the Artist Liu Bolin and we want to continue further our collaboration even further.

3. What do you think is the contribution of China to the art industry? How about the future? It's pretty massive... but I am not an expert of the art industry .

4. What do the Chinese people must learn from western art industry, and what do you think you can learn from other Chinese artists?

It would be appreciated to receive the interview with high res and exclusive portrait of you, also beautiful pictures and profiles of art works.
