JR CLOSE-UP·特写




Inspirational French street artist and photographer, however JR would much rather be labeled as an Urban Artivist. Creating street art since he was a teenager, JR has been all around the world producing beautiful works that reflect humanity. His most used medium is Portrait photography flyposted in public places because he believes that his art will reach more people as opposed to being featured in a museum. His art creates a space for viewers of his work to interact with what they see, and because he photographs local everyday people he diligently binds the viewer with the subject. He is the creator of the Inside Out Project and his goal is to show the world a different point of view than what they see on the television or on the web.

1. What kind of lifestyle defines better JR ? the Art of "story traveling" ...

2. How does your lifestyle contribute to your work?
I am surround myself by friends and artists and it have an incredible impact on my work as I always love implicating people into my process and doing collaboration!

3. What are your long-term ambitions (life and work)?
I love not knowing what tomorrow will be made of. Not knowing Whats next is my favorite sport...

4. Which places where you travel to work are the most impressive ones, and why?
I always travel according of my resources and I am glad I was born this century where you can travel in low coat company if you need to. I always see the city I go as a playground starting from my own town...Paris!

5. Can you talk about the work that you are involved in now?

I am working on the subject of Immigration and just finished a big installation in the abandoned Hospital of Ellis Island in New York... Working on the movement of population of 100 years ago made me want to see the ones of today. I just came back from Sicily and witnessed the arrival of boats with hundreds of immigrants coming from Africa. I am in the middle of it right now... and I cant wait to discover where this will lead me.
